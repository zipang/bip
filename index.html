  • BUILD

    Minify and include it with your flavor of Modernizr

    Download your customized version of Modernizr from here. Then use our build script to concatenate the version of bip at the end of the modernizr script. This way, you'll allways have one and only one script to include in your header, and all the others will be just before your closing </body>

  • PLAY

    Choose your backgrounds

    You could have a lot of fun and surprises digging into ColourLovers vaults. More than 3 millions of repeatable patterns await you.

  • INCLUDE

    Include the bip script in your header section, then preload your images

        <script src="js/bip.js"></script>
    <script>
        bip.preload(['bg1.png', 'bg2.png', 'bg3.png']);
    </script>

  • SPREAD

    Use the script on every new project you start with our HTML5 boilerplate and spread the world
